# 📘 Day 3: Chunking – Important Questions & Answers

---

## ❓ 1. Why is Chunking important?

Chunking is important because searching over large documents can lead to irrelevant results. By dividing text into smaller chunks, the system can retrieve more precise and relevant information, improving the overall quality of responses.

---

## ❓ 2. What is the impact of Chunk Size?

Chunk size plays a critical role in performance:

* If chunks are too large:

  * Retrieval becomes slow
  * Irrelevant information is included

* If chunks are too small:

  * Context is lost
  * Information becomes incomplete

👉 Therefore, a balanced chunk size is necessary for optimal performance.

---

## ❓ 3. Why do we use Overlapping in Chunking?

Overlapping is used to preserve context between chunks. It ensures that important information at the boundaries of chunks is not lost, improving retrieval accuracy.

---

## ❓ 4. What is the role of Chunking in RAG?

Chunking plays a crucial role in RAG systems. It directly affects retrieval quality, which impacts the final response generated by the LLM.

👉 Better chunking → Better retrieval → Better output

---

## 🎯 Summary

* Chunking improves retrieval accuracy
* Proper chunk size is essential
* Overlap helps maintain context
* Chunking is a key component in RAG systems
